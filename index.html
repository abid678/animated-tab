ABOUT

Replace liquid dishwashing detergent, hand soap and general cleaning products with this powerful multipurpose bar. It's made out of Organic Soapberries and Pink Grapefruit Essential Oil that leaves a clean fresh scent. This highly effective soap leaves your glassware and ceramics sparkling clean without compromising the environment. It also makes a fantastic hand soap and general cleaner. Best of all, it's plant-based, vegan, cruelty-free, waste-free and palm oil-free.

INGREDIENTS

Saponified coconut oil (Sodium cocoate), Sodium cocoyl isethionate, Sodium bicarbonate, Pink Grapefruit essential oil (CITRUS PARADISI), Soapberries (Sapindus, mukorossi pericarp)

HOW TO USE

Rub your cloth on the CHUNKY BLOCK, or run under water when filling your sink to impart a natural, streak-free dishwashing soap. Leaves glassware, ceramics and plastics sparkling clean without harming the environment.
